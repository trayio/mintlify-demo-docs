# Overview

## Overview

Domo brings together all your people, data, and systems into one place for a digitally-connected business.

## Authentication

When using the Domo connector, the first thing you will have to do is click on 'New Authentication' in the step editor:

Ensure you correctly add your Domo credentials correctly otherwise operations will not be able to be ran correctly.

## Available Operations

The examples below show one or two of the available connector operations in use.

Please see the [Full Operations Reference](#operationsFull) at the end of this page for details on all available operations for this connector.

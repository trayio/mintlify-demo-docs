# Overview

## Overview

Mailboxlayer enables you to thoroughly check and verify email addresses right at the point of entry into your system.

## Authentication

When using the Mailboxlayer connector, the first thing you will have to do is click on 'New Authentication' in the step editor and add your Mailboxlayer API key, obtained from the Mailboxlayer Admin UI:

## Example - Check mail

This example will walkthrough how to check and verify an email address. The first step is to select the Check email operation from the operations list. Add the email address in the 'Email' input and press 'Run Workflow'.

# Overview

## Overview

The email address to trigger your workflow will be trigger+[ID]@traymail.io where [ID] is the routing id of your workflow. 

To obtain the email address of your workflow, you can click on the trigger itself and 'Copy workflow Email':

Any attachments to an email will be available as file objects with expiring URLs to use in subsequent connector steps.
